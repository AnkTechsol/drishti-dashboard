About DRISHTI दृष्टि

The Single Source of Truth

Drishti is an advanced intelligence dashboard by ANKTechSol designed to provide clean, high-density access to global shifts in Artificial Intelligence, Geopolitics, and Armed Conflict.

🛰️ How to Use

Navigating DRISHTI is optimized for speed and analytical depth:
  • Tabs: Quickly switch between domains (AI, Policy, War) using the top navigation bar.
  • Interactive Data: Every leaderboard row and KPI card is clickable. Tap them to reveal "Tactical Pointers" and underlying intelligence.
  • Live Feed: Head over to the News panels to access direct links to high-authority source hubs like Reuters World and BBC News.

⚙️ How to Customize

Tailor DRISHTI for your personal or professional watchtrack:
  • Settings Panel: Use the "Settings" tab to toggle the Live Ticker, Map Blips, or UI Refresh rates.
  • Persistence: Your settings are saved automatically to your device via `localStorage`. No login is required.
  • Substitute for Terminals: This dashboard is designed to be a local substitute for high-end terminals (Bloomberg/LSEG). All content is powered by ANKTechSol.

☸ The ANKTechSol Vision

Drishti (Vision) is built on the philosophy of information democratization. In an era of fragmented news and algorithmic bias, we provide a unified interface that pulls from the world's most reputable agencies.

No tracking. No ads. No bias. Just Intelligence.
